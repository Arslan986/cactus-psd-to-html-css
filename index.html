How It Works

This is how the PSD to HTML magic happens. No automated shortcuts. All hand-coded to your custom needs.

1. Review

We check out your designs (PSD, Sketch, AI etc...) and specs. Then we address any questions and confirm your requirements (e.g. interactions)

2. Quote

Our project manager works alongside our lead developer to ensure an accurate, timely quote before sending to you for approval.

3. Code

With your go-ahead we hand-code the HTML/CSS aiming to be pixel perfect. Then we implement any custom JavaScript or CMS, if applicable.

4. Deliver

Finally, the developer, dedicated QA staff, and PM review the work. We provide a link to a live preview, then deliver final files via Dropbox.
